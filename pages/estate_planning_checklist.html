Recommended Action Timeline

Immediate (Within 1 Month)

  • • Schedule consultation with estate planning attorney
  • • Gather important documents and financial information
  • • Create basic will if none exists
  • • Update beneficiary designations

Short-term (1-3 Months)

  • • Complete estate planning documents
  • • Establish power of attorney and healthcare directives
  • • Consider trust structures if appropriate
  • • Begin family discussions about estate plans

Long-term (Ongoing)

  • • Review and update plans annually
  • • Adjust for life changes and new assets
  • • Coordinate with financial and tax professionals
  • • Maintain open communication with family

Need Professional Help?

Estate planning can be complex. Consider working with qualified professionals to ensure your plan is comprehensive and legally sound.
