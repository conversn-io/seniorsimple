How Reverse Mortgages Work

Convert home equity into cash without monthly payments

Remain in your home and retain ownership

Loan becomes due when you move or pass away

Proceeds are tax-free and don't affect Social Security

Payout Options

Lump Sum: Receive all proceeds at once

Monthly Payments: Fixed payments for life

Line of Credit: Access funds as needed

Combination: Mix of monthly payments and credit line

Eligibility Requirements

Age 62 or older
Own your home outright or have low mortgage balance
Primary residence requirement
Demonstrate financial capacity
Complete HUD counseling session
Keep home in good condition

Important Considerations

Associated Costs

  • • Origination fees (up to $6,000)
  • • Mortgage insurance premiums
  • • Appraisal and inspection fees
  • • Closing costs and attorney fees
  • • Annual servicing fees

Key Factors

  • • Interest compounds over time
  • • May affect inheritance
  • • Property taxes and insurance required
  • • Home maintenance responsibilities
  • • Potential impact on government benefits
