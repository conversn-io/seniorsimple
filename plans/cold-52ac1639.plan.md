# B2B Compliant Cold Outreach: Enrichment & Export

## Overview

Execute Phase 2 enrichment for AL (priority) and Cold segments, focusing on B2B compliance: business emails and business phones only. Export unified cold list to Smartlead and business phones to GHL, with cell/personal phones held separately pending approval.

## Current State

- ✅ Phase 1 Complete: BigQuery warehouse loaded (275,827 records)
- ✅ Compliance analysis done: identified business vs. cell/personal phone split
- ✅ Cloud Run proxy endpoint added (`/cold-outreach/email-enrich`)
- 🔄 Ready for Phase 2A & 2B enrichment

## Phase 2A: AL Segment (Export Only - No Enrichment Needed)

### Step 1: Export AL to Smartlead & GHL

**Rationale:** AL data comes from AudienceLab and already includes high-quality b2b_email, title, company, and phone data. Skip enrichment and export directly.

Export from BigQuery view with enriched data:

**Smartlead AL:**

```sql
SELECT 
  first_name, last_name, 
  best_email as email,
  company, title,
  'Summit-AL' as custom_1,
  source_id as custom_2
FROM `prospect-warehouse.leads_data.v_advisorist_cold_outreach`
WHERE source_id = 'AL_high_intent'
  AND best_email IS NOT NULL 
  AND best_email LIKE '%@%'
```

Export to: `smartlead_AL.csv` (~20,612 records)

**GHL Business Phone AL:**

```sql
SELECT 
  first_name as firstName, 
  last_name as lastName,
  best_email as email,
  direct_phone_e164 as phone,
  company, title,
  source_id as source_tag,
  'Summit-BusinessPhone-AL' as segment_tag
FROM `prospect-warehouse.leads_data.v_advisorist_cold_outreach`
WHERE source_id = 'AL_high_intent'
  AND direct_phone_e164 IS NOT NULL 
  AND direct_phone_e164 != ''
```

Export to: `ghl_business_phone_AL.csv` (estimated 3,500-5,000 post-enrichment)

**GHL Cell Backup AL (HOLD - DO NOT DIAL):**

```sql
SELECT 
  first_name as firstName, 
  last_name as lastName,
  best_email as email,
  COALESCE(mobile_phone_e164, personal_phone_e164) as phone,
  company, title,
  source_id as source_tag,
  'Summit-CellBackup-AL-DoNotDial' as segment_tag
FROM `prospect-warehouse.leads_data.v_advisorist_cold_outreach`
WHERE source_id = 'AL_high_intent'
  AND (mobile_phone_e164 IS NOT NULL OR personal_phone_e164 IS NOT NULL)
  AND (mobile_phone_e164 != '' OR personal_phone_e164 != '')
```

Export to: `ghl_cell_backup_AL.csv` (~6,806 records)

```sql

FROM `prospect-warehouse.leads_data.v_advisorist_cold_outreach`
```
